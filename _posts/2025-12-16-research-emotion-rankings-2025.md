**Positive Magnitudes:**
- **10**: Life-changing moment, career-defining success
- **8-9**: Major milestone, significant achievement
- **6-7**: Solid win, meaningful progress
- **4-5**: Good day, small victory
- **1-3**: Mild positive, subtle satisfaction

**Negative Magnitudes:**
- **-10**: Devastating blow, questioning everything
- **-8 to -9**: Major setback, significant disappointment
- **-6 to -7**: Rough period, challenging times
- **-4 to -5**: Bad day, frustrating obstacle
- **-1 to -3**: Minor annoyance, small hiccup
